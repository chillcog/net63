# Welcome to Net63 Docs

Dive into the world of cybersecurity, privacy, and the hidden corners of the internet with Net63 Docs. This blog-style documentation site covers essential topics for staying safe online, from basic security practices to advanced dark web navigation.

## Sections

### [Security](Security/)
Learn the fundamentals of online security:
- [SSH Keys](Security/ssh-keys.md) - Secure remote access with SSH.
- [PGP Keys](Security/pgp-key.md) - Encrypt emails and files with PGP.
- [Malware Protection](Security/malware-protection.md) - Defend against viruses and malware.
- [VPNs](Security/vpns.md) - Protect your privacy with virtual private networks.

### [Dark Web](dark-web/)
Explore the anonymous side of the web:
- [Introduction](dark-web/dark-web-introduction.md) - What is the dark web and how to access it safely.
- [Anonymity](dark-web/dark-web-anonymity.md) - Maintain your privacy with Tor and OPSEC.
- [Payments](dark-web/dark-web-payments.md) - Handle cryptocurrencies and escrow securely.
- [Communication](dark-web/dark-web-communication.md) - Secure messaging and email on the dark web.
- [Best Practices](dark-web/dark-web-best-practices.md) - Tips for safe navigation and risk mitigation.
